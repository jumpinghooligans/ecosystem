Aeration Technologies

Ecosystem Consulting Service, Inc. is the only professional lake and reservoir management company that offers all available aeration and oxygenation technologies!


As a lake, reservoir, or pond becomes more productive because of increasing nutrient supply (a process called "eutrophication") the increase in organic matter results in greater consumption of oxygen in respiration. Eventually oxygen consumption exceeds the input rate, resulting in an anaerobic environment. Although a "consequence" of eutrophication, complete oxygen loss leads to increased nutrient input from bottom sediments, loss of important habitat leading to food-web imbalance, accumulation of anaerobic respiration products like iron, manganese, and hydrogen sulfide, and a variety of other impacts. Eutrophication becomes an accelerating cycle! There are many approaches to subsidizing dissolved oxygen to maintain water quality and habitat suitability. Selecting the best aeration approach is critical.

Flow Routing Systems

This technology selects the depth to which tributary inflow enters a lake or reservoir, or from which water leaves over the spillway or to a water supply intake. The approach requires no pump or siphon (no power - it is gravity-driven). The method manages depth-discrete flushing rates to prevent oxygen loss, nutrient build-up, and other causes of algae blooms and water quality problems. (US Patent 4,669,914)
