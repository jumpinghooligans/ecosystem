Overview (1A)

Our science-based approach results in effective and innovative resource quality improvement

  • A Winter Temperature Manipulation to control impacts of land-locked alewife
  • An Aeration Technology that uses photosynthesis as an oxygen source,
  • A Nutrient Inactivation Technology that uses iron generated by anaerobic respiration in a lake,
  • A Gravity-driven Flow Routing Technology for depth-selective inflow or outflow,
  • A Downflow Contactor Oxygenation Technology that requires no in-lake mechanical or electrical parts.

In 1982, a Division of ECS,Inc. was created to assist with the design, fabrication, installation, and maintenance of implementation projects. Our expertise in Applied Limnology has taken us from coast-to-coast, and internationally as far away as Sao Paulo, Brazil. 

Ecosystem Consulting Service, Inc. - Company Profile
Ecosystem Consulting Service, Inc. was founded in 1980 by Dr. Robert W. Kortmann, Ph.D. (Environmental Science, Rutgers University; Limnology/Ecosystem Ecology University of Connecticut). ECS, Inc. and its professional staff continue to maintain its links to the academic community and private sector through its active participation in:

American Water Works Association AWWA
North American Lake Management Society NALMS
American Society of Limnology and Oceanography ASLO
American Fisheries Society AFS


ECS,Inc. consists of two divisions: Research Division - which conducts problem solving diagnostic-feasibility studies, monitoring programs, client-based monitoring programs, method and apparatus development and design. EcoLogical Technologies (EcoTech) - which provides specialized apparatus, implementation, and operation/maintenance services.

Our Mission is to Improve Lake and Water Supply Reservoir Quality!
To provide cost-conscious, state-of-the-art water resource management, including:
Research, Consulting, Watershed Management, Apparatus, Implementation, Operation & Maintenance, and Monitoring
Water Resource Management that works in concert with Nature
