Cooperative Research

ECS, Inc. established the Research Division to diagnose causes of lake and reservoir water quality degradation, eutrophication impacts, and habitat loss and to prescribe effective management approaches.

We welcome the opportunity to participate on Project Teams to diagnose water resource problems and prescribe effective treatments. We have participated on AWWA Expert Panels and as Subcontractor to Environmental Engineering Companies to provide limnological expertise. We have served on a variety of research teams with Universities and Government Environmental Agencies.

For Diagnostic Studies and Long-Term Monitoring Programs we often arrange Client-Based Cooperative Sampling Programs which actively involve Water Utility Staff and Lake Association Members in data collection. That reduces cost substantially. It provides the opportunity to aquire data and information that would otherwise be very difficult or cost-prohibitive. Most importantly, involvement of Clients in the project leads to an improved understanding of the resource and management options. Informed decisions by the Client are the result.
